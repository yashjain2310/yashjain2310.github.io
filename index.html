My Story

Meet Yash Jain, an inspiring entrepreneur who embarked on an exciting journey in 2024 by launching their own wall paint and texture business. A national-level basketball player, Yash has always embraced the thrill of competition and the importance of teamwork, qualities that now fuel their entrepreneurial spirit.

Graduating from the prestigious Mithibai College in Mumbai, Yash developed a keen eye for design and aesthetics, which became a driving force behind their business. Their creativity was further nurtured through a diploma in dance from the Terence Lewis Professional Training Institute, where they learned to appreciate the artistry of movement and expression.

In addition to their artistic talents, Yash brings a wealth of corporate experience to the table, having honed skills in graphic design, video editing, and digital marketing. This background enables them to create engaging marketing strategies that resonate with clients, making their brand stand out in a competitive market.

Driven by a passion for transforming spaces, Yash is dedicated to providing high-quality products and services that inspire creativity and elevate everyday environments. With an innovative mindset and a commitment to excellence, they aim to help people see the beauty in their surroundings, one wall at a time.
